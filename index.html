About

We are artists working together since 2015. Coming from rural childhoods, we explore land art and environmental art by combining natural materials, site-specific installations, and reflections on Ukrainian pastoral traditions within contemporary contexts. For us, pastoral life is both an ideal and a fragile utopia in today’s Ukraine at war.

Selected Works

Projects

Recent projects include site-specific installations in quarries, riverbeds, and village landscapes, reflecting on the fertile connections between human presence and the land. Many of our works focus on the Mogrytsia quarry in Sumy region, where the “Border Space” land art symposium took place before the war.
