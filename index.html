About Us

The creative association has existed since 2015, from the first joint project created at the winter festival of land art Mithogenesis (Vinnytsia, Ukraine). Both participants - Marharyta Zhurunova from Vinnytsia (29.11.1994) and Bohdan Lokatyr from Lviv (04.05.1993) studied together at the Ukrainian Academy of Printing and became partners and co-authors.

The main activities of the duo are land, environmental and site specific art. The preferred theme is the mutual influence of the person and the landscape, toxic manifestations of it. Of course, after February 24, 2022, most of the works created are on the theme of war.
