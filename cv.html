CV

The creative association has existed since 2015, from the first joint project created at the winter festival of land art Mythogenesis (Vinnytsia, Ukraine). Both participants — Marharyta Zhurunova (Vinnytsia, 29.11.1994) and Bohdan Lokatyr (Lviv, 04.05.1993) — studied together at the Ukrainian Academy of Printing and became partners and co-authors.

The main activities of the duo are land art, environmental art, and site-specific art. The preferred themes are the mutual influence of person and landscape and its toxic manifestations. Since February 24, 2022, most works have addressed the theme of war.

In 2019, both artists became laureates of the President of Ukraine scholarships for young writers and artists and also received a grant from the President of Ukraine.
